Exercise 1.

Xt=(1-t)⁢Bt1-t,t∈[0,1).

Exercise 2.

Let (Bs,s≥0) be a Brownian motion. For every t≥0, define

gt:=sup⁡{s∈[0,t]:Bs=0},dt=inf⁡{s≥t:Bs=0}.

Show that the pair (gt,dt) has the same law as (t⁢g1,t⁢d1).
